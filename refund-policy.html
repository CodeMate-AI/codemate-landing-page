Refund Policy for CodeMate

In case you aren't satisfied with the product, you can raise a refund request by sending an email to contact@codemate.ai. Refund requests must take place within 14 days of the purchase. Any requests after 14 days of the purchase will be considered invalid. For all valid refund requests, we will refund the deducted amount (except payment gateway fees) in 7-14 business days.
